    RECIPE 5: STAWBERRY MILK-SHAKE

    INGREDIENTS:

  • 250 g Strawberries Hulled
  • ▢150 ml Milk Whole
  • ▢200 g Ice Cream Vanilla
  • ▢1 tsp Vanilla Extract

    • INSTRUCTIONS:

    1. Place the hulled strawberries, milk, vanilla ice cream, sugar, and vanilla extract into a blender.
    2. Blend until the mixture is smooth.
    3. Pour the smooth mixture into two glasses.
    4. Serve and enjoy your Strawberry Milkshake immediately.
